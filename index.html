Vincenza's Italian Bakery

Delicious and freshly made Italian food. That's what is waiting for you at Vincenza's Italian Bakery. Conveniently located by Yonge and Sheppard, our family has proudly served North York and the GTA for decades. Come enjoy an atmosphere that will remind you that you're part of the family too.
